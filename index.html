Abstract

This project focuses on addressing the critical issue of food pollution, its sources, and potential solutions to reduce its impact on public health and the environment.

Existing System

The current systems to handle food pollution are inadequate, leading to health issues and environmental degradation.

Proposed System

Our proposed solution includes monitoring and reducing pollutants in food production and advocating for policy changes.

Advantages

  • Improves public health by reducing contaminants in food.
  • Promotes sustainable practices in agriculture and food production.

Disadvantages

  • High implementation costs.
  • Possible resistance from industrial sectors.

Conclusion

The project underlines the importance of reducing food pollution to protect public health and the environment.
